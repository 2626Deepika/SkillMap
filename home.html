Skill Map

A skill map is a structured visual or analytical representation of an individual’s current competencies, proficiency levels, and skill gaps relative to a target role or industry standard. It categorizes skills into domains such as technical, analytical, and soft skills, and assigns measurable levels (e.g., beginner, intermediate, advanced). In a resume analysis system, a skill map helps identify strengths, highlight missing competencies, and recommend targeted learning paths. By aligning existing skills with job requirements, a skill map enables strategic upskilling, clearer career planning, and improved employability outcomes

Resume Analyzer

A resume analyser provides intelligent course recommendations based on a candidate’s current skill level, experience, and identified gaps. By evaluating technical skills, certifications, projects, and industry alignment, it determines whether the candidate falls into beginner, intermediate, or advanced categories. For beginners, it suggests foundational courses to strengthen core concepts and essential tools. For intermediate candidates, it recommends skill-enhancement programs, project-based learning, and domain-specific certifications. For advanced professionals, it proposes specialized courses, leadership training, and emerging technology programs to maintain a competitive edge. This level-based recommendation approach ensures structured skill development and improves overall employability.

Course Recommendation

A good course recommendation system suggests learning paths that match your current skill level and long-term goals. For beginners, it recommends foundational courses that build core knowledge, introduce essential tools, and establish strong fundamentals. At intermediate levels, it suggests courses that deepen understanding, introduce real-world projects, and expand technical skills. For advanced learners, it recommends specialized or expert-level courses that focus on complex topics, performance optimization, industry best practices, and mastery of cutting-edge technologies. By tailoring recommendations to your level, you can learn efficiently, avoid overwhelm, and progress steadily toward your career objectives
